Indicadores de especulación
===========================

could/MD dentro de un VP -> MD VP hace que el VP grande sea una especulación.
"could mimic some differentiation/activation stimuli allowing nuclear NF-KB expression." S1.7
"could suppress the basal-level activity of the conalbumin promoter in both Jurkat and HeLa cells."
"could transactivate through its cognate motif."
"could contribute to nuclear signaling mechanisms that regulate a specific program of monocyte differentiation."

could/MD dentro de un VP, que está dentro de un S que dice "One explanation for", hace que el S sea la especulación.S24.6
"One explanation for the perpetuation of virus infection in macrophages could be sustained nuclear NF-kappa B expression."


could/MD dentro de un VP, que a su vez está dentro de un S que comienza con No/DT, no es especulación?
"No relationship could be established between glucocorticoid receptor binding and antidepressant medication."


(MD= Modal) 
(DT=Determiner)
